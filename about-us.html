What a Great Place for Your Child to Start

"Dream Discover Deliver".

Precious Thorn provides strong social and educational foundations that prepare your child for success in early childhood and beyond. Your child will learn from highly qualified teachers and benefit from interacting with other children. Children work together, have constructive fun together, and discover how enjoyable learning can be.

Why we are unique:

  • Smart Learning Tools
  • Learner Centered Approach
  • 3c's - Confidence, Communication, Creativity
  • Nurturing Each Childs Abilities
